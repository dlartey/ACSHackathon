LIBDO - Facillitating Green Growth in Developing Countries

Our Mission

At LIBDO, we are focused on pairing startups in developing countries with foreign investors without red-tape

We aim to help countries grow, by reducing the brain drain & parnerships with micro-loans

How do we achieve this? Through a 3 step plan

Step 1

Complete our questionnaire to assess your suitability to be linked with a startup in a LEDS

Step 2

Review the results of your questionnaire to see whether you would be a good match

Step 3

If suitable, we will contact you to discuss how you can provide renewable energy & micro-loans to companies & the benefits this would provide to you
