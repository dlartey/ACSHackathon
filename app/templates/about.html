ZenZone

What We Do?

In a world so technologically intertwined, digital immersion can have us more disconnected than ever. People of all ages are finding themselves unpredictably demoralised and demotivated.The ability the compare and consume online content indiscriminately has destroyed self esteem and self control.Here at ZenZone, we know that online wellbeing has become a critical emotional metric that is able to affect us in unprecedented ways . We aim to create a quantifiable emotional assessment centre (i.e. the ZenZone) that determines an area in a User's life requiring attention. Users are then provided with new, tailored links to tools that give them a place to start.

Three Dimension of ZenZone Algorithm

Step 1: Zenzone Assessment

Our Zenzone assessment includes a bank of over 20 question designed to improve a Users wellbeing by locating ares for development. The assessment tests for signifcant lifestyle deficiencies but focus predominatly on online wellbeing.

Step 2: Creating Zenzone Recommendation

Our Zenzone algorithm uses a score-based system to provide feedback of user wellbeing condition. It prvides suggestions and guidlines to people about their mental condition and online concious.

Making Change

Users are providd with an area of their life to develop, and access to randomly - generated relevent tools.
