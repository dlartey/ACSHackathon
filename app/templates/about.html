ZenZone

Our mission

In a world so technologically intertwined, digital immersion can have us more disconnected than ever. People of all ages are finding themselves unpredictably demoralised and demotivated

The ability the compare and consume online content indiscriminately has destroyed self esteem and self control.

Here at ZenZone, we know that online wellbeing has become a critical emotional metric that is able to affect us in unprecedented ways . We aim to create a quantifiable emotional assessment centre (i.e. the ZenZone) that determines an area in a User's life requiring attention. Users are then provided with new, tailored links to tools that give them a place to start.
